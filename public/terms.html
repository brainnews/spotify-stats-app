Privacy Policy

What We Collect

BuyMoreMusic connects to your Spotify account to display your listening statistics. We collect:

  • Your Spotify username and profile information
  • Your top artists, albums, and tracks
  • Temporary authentication tokens to access Spotify's API

What We Do With Your Data

We do NOT store your listening data on our servers. All statistics are fetched directly from Spotify's API and displayed to you in real-time.

We use your data to:

  • Display your personalized music statistics
  • Generate purchase links to music platforms (Bandcamp, Amazon Music, Apple Music)
  • Store your preferences locally in your browser (theme, purchased albums, etc.)

Local Storage

We store the following information locally in your browser using localStorage:

  • Theme preference (dark/light mode)
  • Time range selection (4 weeks, 6 months, all time)
  • Purchased albums list (honor system, stored locally only)
  • Section collapse/expand states

This data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser data.

What We Don't Do

  • We do NOT sell your data to third parties
  • We do NOT share your listening history with anyone
  • We do NOT track you across other websites
  • We do NOT store your music statistics in a database
  • We do NOT send marketing emails

Third-Party Services

BuyMoreMusic uses the following third-party services:

  • Spotify API: To fetch your listening statistics. See Spotify's Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare: For hosting and SSL. See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy

Cookies

We use minimal cookies only for:

  • Maintaining your Spotify authentication session
  • Storing access and refresh tokens (required for Spotify API access)

These cookies expire after 1 hour and are automatically refreshed while you're using the app.
